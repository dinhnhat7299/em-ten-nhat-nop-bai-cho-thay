OVERVIEW

LOKI

Loki, Prince of Asgard, Odinson, rightful heir of Jotunheim, and God of Mischief, is burdened with glorious purpose.
His desire to be a king drives him to sow chaos in Asgard. In his lust for power, he extends his reach to Earth.

LOKI
(TV Show,2021)

In Marvel Studios’ “Loki,” the mercurial villain Loki (Tom Hiddleston)
resumes his role as the God of Mischief in a new series that takes place
after the events of “Avengers: Endgame.” Kate Herron directs and
Michael Waldron is head writer. Debuts on Disney+ in June 9, 2021.
